AI Prompt Helper

This powerful prompt helps you convert your notes, textbook paragraphs, or any information into the required Q&A format using an AI chatbot.

How to Use:

  1. Click "Copy Prompt" and paste it into an AI like ChatGPT or Google Gemini.
  2. To use your own text: Replace the placeholder `[PASTE YOUR FULL TEXT HERE...]` with your paragraph or notes.
  3. To use a general topic: Delete the placeholder text and just type a topic like `Topic: The laws of thermodynamics`.
  4. Copy the AI's response back into this tool's "Markdown Input" tab.

If I provide a block of text, you MUST base the questions and answers STRICTLY on the information within that text only. Do not use external knowledge. 5.
